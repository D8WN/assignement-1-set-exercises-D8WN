Brooklyn Nine-Nine

Brooklyn Nine-Nine is a critically acclaimed American police procedural comedy television series. Set in the fictional 99th precinct of the NYPD in Brooklyn, the show centers around a team of detectives headed by the serious and stoic Captain Raymond Holt.

The story primarily follows Jake Peralta, a brilliant but immature detective whose antics are balanced by his ambitious and rule-abiding partner Amy Santiago. The ensemble cast also includes the emotionally unpredictable Rosa Diaz, food-loving Charles Boyle, the fiercely competent Terry Jeffords, and the quirky administrator Gina Linetti.

  • Total Seasons: 8

  • Notable Awards: Golden Globe for Best Comedy Series (2014), GLAAD Media Awards

Main Cast & Characters

  • Andy Samberg as Jake Peralta - A talented but childish detective with a love for Die Hard and a knack for solving crimes.
  • Andre Braugher as Captain Raymond Holt - The stoic, openly gay captain who commands respect with his deadpan delivery.
  • Stephanie Beatriz as Rosa Diaz - A tough, mysterious detective known for her intimidating personality and personal privacy.
  • Melissa Fumero as Amy Santiago - A type-A overachiever and Jake's eventual wife.
  • Terry Crews as Terry Jeffords - The strong yet gentle sergeant who loves yogurt and protects his team like family.
  • Joe Lo Truglio as Charles Boyle - Jake's overly loyal best friend and a quirky, food-obsessed detective.
  • Chelsea Peretti as Gina Linetti - The precinct's eccentric civilian administrator with a talent for sarcasm and social media.

Critical Reception & Reviews

"Brooklyn Nine-Nine is one of the smartest, funniest and most consistently enjoyable shows on television." - The Guardian

"A sitcom with heart, cleverness, and inclusivity. The chemistry among the cast is undeniable." - IndieWire

The show has earned a cult following and an impressive 97% audience score on Rotten Tomatoes. Critics and fans alike praise it for its progressive storytelling, genuine representation of LGBTQ+ characters, and its ability to deliver laugh-out-loud comedy alongside meaningful commentary.
